THE OVERLOOKED DEMOGRAPHIC

There is a significant demographic of women today who have had to put their careers on hold for primarily family reasons - motherhood, marital agreements, and other domestic reasons. These women, after so long away from the workforce, face a challenging climb in reintegrating.
